> "All Scripture is breathed out by God and profitable for teaching, for reproof, for correction, and for training in righteousness." — 2 Timothy 3:16

The Bible is the written Word of God — 66 books in the Protestant canon (Catholic and Orthodox canons include additional Old Testament books) — authored by approximately 40 human writers over 1,500 years, yet unified by a single divine Author and a single redemptive storyline pointing to Jesus Christ.

## Key Topics

- [**Authority of Scripture**](/know-yhwh/scripture/authority/) — The divine origin, self-attesting nature, and supreme authority of God's Word
- [**Inspiration & Inerrancy**](/know-yhwh/scripture/inspiration-and-inerrancy/) — How God produced Scripture and why it is trustworthy
- [**The Canon**](/know-yhwh/scripture/canon/) — How the books of the Bible were recognized and received
- [**Hermeneutics**](/know-yhwh/scripture/hermeneutics/) — Principles for rightly interpreting the Word of God
- [**Old Testament**](/know-yhwh/scripture/old-testament/) — Law, Prophets, and Writings
- [**New Testament**](/know-yhwh/scripture/new-testament/) — Gospels, Epistles, and Revelation
