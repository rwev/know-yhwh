> "The name of the LORD is a strong tower; the righteous man runs into it and is safe." — Proverbs 18:10

In the ancient Near East, a name was not merely a label but a revelation of character. Each name God uses for Himself unveils an aspect of His nature, His relationship with His people, or His redemptive purposes.

## Major Names

- [**YHWH**](/know-yhwh/names-of-god/yhwh/) — The covenant name of God, "I AM WHO I AM"
- [**Elohim**](/know-yhwh/names-of-god/elohim/) — God as Creator and sovereign ruler
- [**El Shaddai**](/know-yhwh/names-of-god/el-shaddai/) — God Almighty, the all-sufficient one
- [**Adonai**](/know-yhwh/names-of-god/adonai/) — Lord and Master, sovereign over all
- [**El Elyon**](/know-yhwh/names-of-god/el-elyon/) — God Most High, supreme above all powers
- [**YHWH Yireh**](/know-yhwh/names-of-god/jehovah-jireh/) — The LORD will provide, the God who sees and supplies

## Why Names Matter

When God reveals a name, He is inviting His people into deeper knowledge of who He is. To call upon the *name* of the LORD is to call upon His very person and character (Romans 10:13).
