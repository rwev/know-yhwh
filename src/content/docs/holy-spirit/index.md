> "But the Helper, the Holy Spirit, whom the Father will send in my name, he will teach you all things and bring to your remembrance all that I have said to you." — John 14:26

The Holy Spirit is the third person of the Trinity — fully God, co-equal and co-eternal with the Father and the Son. He is not an impersonal force but a divine person who speaks, teaches, grieves, and intercedes.
