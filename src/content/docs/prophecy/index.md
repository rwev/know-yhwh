> "For no prophecy was ever produced by the will of man, but men spoke from God as they were carried along by the Holy Spirit." — 2 Peter 1:21

Biblical prophecy is God's revelation of His purposes — both near-term declarations to His people and far-reaching visions of the end of all things. Prophecy demonstrates God's sovereignty over history and His faithfulness to His promises.
