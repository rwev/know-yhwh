# Know YHWH — Complete Site Content Design

## Goal

Expand the Know YHWH site from 9 sections (36 pages) to 28 sections covering the full breadth of Christian theology. Deepen all existing pages and write all new content through a 4-agent pipeline ensuring scriptural accuracy, linguistic depth, and theological orthodoxy.

## Theological Approach

- **Mere Christianity / Ecumenical**: Focus on what all orthodox Christians share (Nicene Creed, Chalcedonian Definition, Apostles' Creed)
- Where traditions disagree (predestination, baptism mode, spiritual gifts, church governance), present major views fairly without taking sides
- All claims backed by Scripture references
- No speculative theology — stick to what Scripture clearly teaches
- Hebrew/Greek terms transliterated with original script in parentheses

## Content Standards

- **Page length**: ~60-100 lines of MDX per page
- **Scripture**: 3-5+ key passages per page, using blockquote styling
- **Original languages**: Hebrew/Greek word studies where relevant
- **Church history**: Brief context from creeds, councils, key theologians
- **Cross-references**: Links to related pages on the site

## Agent Pipeline (per section)

Each section passes through 4 specialized agents in sequence:

1. **Editor Agent** — Plans section structure, determines pages, sets headings and topic targets
2. **Biblical Scholar Agent** — Writes the MDX content with deep Scripture exposition
3. **Hebrew/Greek Linguist Agent** — Enriches with original-language word studies, etymologies, translational nuances
4. **Theological Critic Agent** — Reviews against Nicene/Chalcedonian orthodoxy, checks for errors, ensures ecumenical balance
